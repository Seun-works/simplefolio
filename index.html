About me

As a former financial analyst, i have always been good with numbers but i never felt passionate about having a long-term career in it. These past years have led to me really rethinking about my life goals and what it means to have a career doing something you love. I found out about coding from a mentor of mine who thought that it would be a good fit for me. The first day i decided to take his advise seriously was the moment i realised the beauty in creating something from scratch that you can call your own.

Over the past year, i’ve studied books, courses and practiced everything i learn into becoming a better web developer. I find it very intriguing that with just a little effort in learning technical things, you can achieve so much. For example, i have been able to create websites from scratch while adopting a mobile-first workflow and from looking at design images, which undeniably made things a lot easier for me. This can be seen in my loop-studios and sunny-side landing pages.

I am very comfortable with the usual front-end technologies like SASS, Bootstrap and javascript and ReactJs. Currently, i am learning how to use NodeJs and Express to work on the back-end of my projects as this side of software development has piqued my interests. This is my portfolio and i hope it leaves a good impression on you. Thank you and have a lovely day.
